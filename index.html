HUB Young Downloader

How to use:

  1. Open an login in your HUB Young account here.
  2. Open the book you'd like to download in the HUB Young own web reader.
  3. Copy the number from the url https://young.hubscuola.it/viewer/########?page=1 in the Volume ID field.
  4. Now open the developer tools with CTRL + SHIFT + I and go under the application tab, on the list of the left select local storage then where it says https://young.hubscuola.it/ and on the table that will appear look for the tokenSession, click on that. It will appear on the bottom, copy it without the quotation marks and put it in the token field.
  5. You are now ready to download! Just press that button and it will do it all, might take a while tho.
