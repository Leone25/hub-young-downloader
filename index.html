How to use:

  1. Open an login in your HUB Young account here.
  2. Open the book you'd like to download in the HUB Young own web reader.
  3. Copy the number from the url("https://young.hubscuola.it/viewer/{this thing}?page=1") in the volume id.
  4. Now open the developer tools with CTRL + SHIFT + I and go under the "application" tab, on the list of the left select "local storage" then where it says "https://young.hubscuola.it/" and on the table that will appear look for the "tokenSession", click on that. It will appear on the bottom, copy it without the quotation marks and put it in the token.
  5. You are now ready to download! Just press that button and it will do it all, might take a while tho.

Remember that you are sesponsible for what you are doing on the internet and even tho this website exists it might not be legal in your country to create personal backups of books.
